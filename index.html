The Story So Far...

Chaos Killed is a alternative metal band, originally hailing from Colliers, WV, but calling Pittsburgh, PA their home now.

Chaos Killed got their start at West Liberty University outside of Wheelng, WV where Guitarist/Vocalist Josh Jenkins, Bassist Anthony Rees, and Drummer/Vocalist Josh Revels all took classes. They played various clubs in Wheeling before breaking into the Pittsburgh scene in 2010, opening for bands such as Evans Blue, Periphery, Trapt, 10 Years, and The Red Jumpsuit Apparatus

The band began a hiatus in 2013 after releasing 2 Albums: Blame It All On The Birds in 2009, and Singularity in 2011. In 2016, the band began compiling songs that each member had written on their own and returned to the Wheeling area to play a reunion show in September of that year. They announced that they were starting to write new music, and in 2018, Pathfinder, the first single of the upcoming and, as of yet, untitled third record was released. The song displayed a new, heavier direction for the trio and saw Revels take over as producer.
